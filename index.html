About Us


Since 1999

Our restaurant was built in 1999. The first name of our restaurant was WE LUV DESSERT. In 2009, the founder planned to change the name of the restaurant to ε13. This plan was implemented in 2011. The name of this restaurant has been maintained since now.ε13.

There are various desserts and drinks that we sell. If interested, please continue reading. Our restaurant provides food delivery service for guests. We also have a beautifully decorated physical restaurant in Bandar Seri Botani.
